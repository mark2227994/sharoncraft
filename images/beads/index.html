Founder Story

At 21, I finished school and realized the next step was not clear. The opportunities I expected were closed, and the silence after graduation felt heavy. Instead of waiting for doors to open, I decided to build one.

SharonCraft started as a simple idea: if the world is not hiring, I will create a path where talent can be seen. I partnered with local artisans who hold generations of skill in their hands and gave those skills a modern, premium home.

Today, every piece we release is proof that craft is a career, heritage is powerful, and your beginning does not have to limit your destiny. We build with purpose, and we deliver with pride.

Kelvin Mark, Founder

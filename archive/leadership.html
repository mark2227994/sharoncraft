Leadership

Meet the people leading SharonCraft.

SharonCraft is led by Founder & CEO Kelvin Mark, with Sharon Ruth guiding the brand's creative direction as Lead Designer.

Founder & CEO

Kelvin Mark

Kelvin Mark is the Founder & CEO of SharonCraft and leads the company's direction, operations, growth, and customer experience.

He oversees the brand strategy, digital storefront, catalog experience, customer journey, and the systems that make SharonCraft easy to discover and shop.

SR
Lead Designer

Sharon Ruth

Sharon Ruth is the Lead Designer at SharonCraft, guiding the creative direction, color stories, and handcrafted beadwork style behind the collection.

Her design eye shapes the signature look of SharonCraft across jewelry, bridal sets, gifts, and home decor.

Brand Snapshot

Why this leadership structure matters.

SharonCraft combines business direction and creative craft leadership in one clear structure: Kelvin Mark leads the company overall, while Sharon Ruth leads the design language that gives the brand its distinct handmade identity.

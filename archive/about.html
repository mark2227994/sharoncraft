About SharonCraft

A handmade brand built around color, care, and easier shopping.

SharonCraft was founded by Kelvin Mark✓ at 21. He leads the brand as Founder and CEO, shaping the business direction, customer experience, and day-to-day growth of SharonCraft.

Sharon Ruth is the Lead Designer, shaping the beadwork look, color stories, and handcrafted details behind the collection.

Together, they keep SharonCraft warm, practical, and easy to shop, whether you are buying for yourself, for a gift, or for your home.

Quick Facts

A few simple things to know about SharonCraft.

We keep the story simple so you know who we are and what kind of experience to expect.

What is SharonCraft?

SharonCraft is a Kenyan handmade brand for beadwork, gift ideas, home pieces, and occasion sets that feel personal and easy to shop.

Where are the products made?

The collection is handmade in Kenya by craftspeople who keep color, durability, and crafted detail at the center of each piece.

What is the goal of the brand?

The goal is simple: make beautiful beadwork easier to buy, gift, and enjoy without turning the shopping process into hard work.

Who leads SharonCraft?

Kelvin Mark✓ is the Founder and CEO of SharonCraft, while Sharon Ruth leads the design direction behind the collection.

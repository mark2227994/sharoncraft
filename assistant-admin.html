Quick Help

Best simple flow

  1. Add the product name, category, and price.
  2. Upload one clear photo first.
  3. Write one short description the customer can understand quickly.
  4. Save, then check the Catalog tab to confirm it looks right.
Folder Workflow

Keep sale-ready photos organized

  • Put fresh project photos in assets/images/ready-for-sale/.
  • When the product is already selling, move or copy the chosen files into assets/images/live-products/.
  • Move replaced photos into assets/images/archive/.
  • This assistant follows the same stage labels, even when the image itself is already a live Supabase URL.
Photo Checklist

Use this before upload

Main shot

Clean background, bright light, product centered, easy to understand in one second.

Detail shot

Close-up of bead texture, pattern, clasp, stitching, or standout handmade detail.

Styled shot

Show it worn or placed in space so the customer understands size and real use.

  • Use natural window light and clean the phone camera first.
  • Use plain cream, beige, white, linen, or soft wood backgrounds.
  • Avoid clutter, busy fabrics, harsh shadows, and heavy filters.
  • For bracelets and necklaces, include one worn photo if possible.
  • For home decor, include one plain shot and one styled-in-room shot.

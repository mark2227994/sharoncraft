Help & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, delivery, and our beautiful handmade beadwork.

How do I place an order?

Browse our catalog, add items to your cart, and check out directly via WhatsApp. This allows us to confirm your details, verify availability, and finalize the payment link or M-Pesa details securely.

Do you offer custom beadwork?

Yes, we do! Please message us on WhatsApp with your color preferences or design ideas, and we will do our best to accommodate your request depending on our artisan capacity.

How long does delivery take?

Orders within Nairobi typically take 1-2 business days. Deliveries outside Nairobi via Fargo Courier or preferred bus parcels take 2-4 business days.

How do I pay via M-Pesa?

During the WhatsApp checkout process, we will provide you with the exact Paybill or Till Number, and the total amount including delivery fees. Once paid, you will receive a prompt confirmation.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we focus on deliveries within Kenya. However, contact us directly on WhatsApp for special arrangements for international bulk or wholesale gifts.

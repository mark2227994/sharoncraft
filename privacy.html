Privacy

We keep customer details simple, useful, and respectful.

This page explains what SharonCraft may collect when you browse the website, create an account, track an order, or contact us on WhatsApp.

What We Collect

Only the details needed to run the shop smoothly.

Depending on how you use the site, SharonCraft may collect your name, email address, phone number, delivery area, order details, and messages you send during ordering or support.

The website may also store limited technical information such as page views, product views, cart activity, and device/browser information used to understand how the storefront is performing.

How We Use It

Your information helps us respond, deliver, and improve.

We use customer information to create accounts, support sign-in, respond to WhatsApp requests, prepare orders, confirm delivery details, and improve the shopping experience on the website.

We may also review product and page activity to understand which collections are most useful to visitors and where the website needs improvement.

Account Details

If you register on the site, your account information is used to help you sign in and manage saved customer details more easily.

Orders & Delivery

Order details may be stored so SharonCraft can confirm purchases, track status, and support delivery or follow-up questions.

Analytics

Basic storefront analytics may be collected to understand which pages and products customers use most often.

Storage & Sharing

How information is handled

  • Customer and storefront data may be stored using SharonCraft website tools and hosting services.
  • Information is used for running the shop, not for selling customer data.
  • Details may be shared only when reasonably needed to complete orders, respond to support, or comply with legal obligations.
Your Choices

You can ask questions or request updates

  • You may contact SharonCraft to correct contact details linked to an order or account.
  • You may choose not to create an account and still contact SharonCraft directly on WhatsApp.
  • You can limit some browser storage by clearing cookies or local site data in your browser settings.
Contact About Privacy

If you have a question about your details, reach out directly.

For privacy-related questions, order corrections, or account concerns, contact SharonCraft by email or WhatsApp.

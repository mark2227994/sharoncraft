Product Editor

Beginner quick start

1. Upload or choose a photo. 2. Pick the category and price. 3. Click the AI name helper if you do not know the product name yet. 4. Use the description starter, then edit the words so they sound natural for your product.

The helper gives you a starter name from the category, description, details, and badge. You can always edit the result.

Select a file to save it inside this browser for previews and product cards, or type an `assets/images/...` path if the file already exists in the project.

How to upload a product photo
  1. Click `Choose image from computer` and pick one clear photo.
  2. Wait for the preview image to appear.
  3. If you want more than one photo, add extra image paths in `More gallery image paths` or use the Assets tab.
  4. Use `Selected gallery images` to choose the main photo that customers see first.

Start Here

What the Main Controls Do

The top action buttons affect the whole admin. `Save Catalog` stores your current catalog in this browser, `Restore Default Catalog` returns the starter catalog, `Open Storefront` opens the public site, and the status line under the hero tells you what just changed.

  • Top tab bar: moves you between Workspace, Catalog, Preview, Sales, Orders, Operations, Profit, Social, Customers, Bundles, Replies, Growth, Guide, and Assets.
  • Status line: confirms saves, resets, uploads, and quick actions.
  • Best work order: edit in Workspace, confirm in Catalog, and check the result in Preview.
Product Workflow

Workspace, Catalog, Preview, and Assets

  • Workspace: `New Product` clears the form, `AI Name Helper` suggests a starter name, `Add Description Starter` helps beginners write product copy, and `Save Product` creates or updates one product.
  • Product fields: name, category, price, badge, description, details, featured toggle, and new toggle control how the storefront presents the item.
  • Profit-related fields: mom price, delivery charged, delivery cost, stock source, stock quantity, and reserved quantity help operations and profit tabs stay accurate.
  • Image tools: main image, gallery images, computer upload, and selected gallery controls decide what image appears first and what extra views the customer sees.
  • Catalog: search, category filter, and feature filter help you find products fast. `Edit` sends the product back to Workspace, `Preview` opens it in Preview, and `Delete` removes it from the catalog.
  • Featured Products manager: switches homepage featuring on or off without reopening every product.
  • Preview: page selector changes the page in the phone frame, `Reload Preview` refreshes it, and `Open Full Page` opens that page outside the frame.
  • Assets: the image library shows existing project images. `Use as Main` makes one the main product image, and `Add to Gallery` pushes it into the current product gallery.
Orders and Operations

Daily Selling Tools

  • Orders form: customer name, phone, product, area, quantity, status, and order note capture the full order record.
  • Save Order: stores the order and feeds related summaries such as customer and growth data.
  • Order Snapshot: gives a quick top-level picture of current orders.
  • Order List: lets you update status or delete an order row.
  • Delivery Manager: area name, charge client, and actual delivery cost define delivery zones and their costs.
  • Expense Tracker: records business spending with name, amount, note, and saved history.
  • Stock panel: shows each product source, stock quantity, and reserved quantity to reduce overselling risk.
Profit and Growth

Money Tracking Tools

  • Profit Dashboard: summarizes saved product margin values so you can see what is worth pushing harder.
  • Reset Profit History: clears mom price and delivery values from products. Use it only when you want a true reset.
  • Quick Calculator: product selector, quantity, mom price, my selling price, delivery charged, and actual delivery cost let you test an order before accepting it.
  • Profit Breakdown: converts the calculator numbers into cost total, selling total, product profit, delivery profit, and total profit.
  • Growth form: target amount, amount saved, and goal note track the bigger business milestone you are saving toward.
  • Growth metrics, goal card, and monthly view: show whether current business activity is moving you toward your kiosk goal.
Social and Replies

Marketing and Fast Messaging

  • Social links: WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok fields control the public social links on the storefront.
  • Caption Studio: product selector, tone selector, `Generate Caption`, `Copy Caption`, and caption box help you prepare social posts faster.
  • Weekly Planner: stores daily themes and notes for the content calendar.
  • Social Ready Products: lets you load strong products straight into the caption workflow.
  • Top Chat Drivers: highlights which products are most likely to pull shopper attention.
  • Replies form: template title, category, and reply message build reusable WhatsApp messages.
  • Useful Placeholders: tokens like `[Customer Name]` remind you what to personalize before sending.
  • Reply list: `Copy` sends a template to clipboard, `Edit` loads it back into the form, and `Delete` removes it.
Customers and Bundles

Relationship and Offer Tools

  • Customers tab: customer metrics, customer list, and best relationship view help you spot repeat buyers and what they like most.
  • Bundles form: bundle name, price, note, and product picker help you package multiple products into one stronger offer.
  • Save Bundle: stores the new bundle or updates the bundle currently being edited.
  • Bundle Summary and Bundle List: show how many bundle offers you have, what is inside them, and which ones need editing or removal.
Dashboards

Sales and Summary Views

  • Sales metrics: summarize revenue, clicks, and units in one place.
  • Sales chart and sales table: help you compare products visually and in row form.
  • Reset Sales to 0: clears sample sales values across products when you want to start fresh.
  • Snapshots across the admin: order snapshot, bundle summary, customer highlight, goal card, and draft card are mini dashboards for quick decisions.
Business Tips

Simple Rules to Keep Nearby

1. Product Profit

If your mom says a product is `1000` and you sell it online for `1500`, your product profit is `500`.

2. Delivery Profit

If you charge the client `500` for delivery and the real delivery costs `200`, your delivery profit is `300`.

3. Total Profit

Total profit = product profit + delivery profit. This is the main number to watch before opening your own kiosk.

4. Safe Pricing

Always check that your selling price covers your mom price, packaging, transport, and your own margin.

5. Small Batch Thinking

Start with the products that bring the highest profit and move fast. Grow from what is already working.

6. Your Goal

Use the website to learn which products sell well, build steady profit, then reinvest into your own kiosk step by step.

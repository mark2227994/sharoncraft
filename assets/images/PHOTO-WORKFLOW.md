# SharonCraft Product Photo Workflow

Use these folders to keep product photos easy to understand:

- `assets/images/ready-for-sale/`
  Fresh product photos that are almost ready to use.
- `assets/images/live-products/`
  Photos for products that are already on sale.
- `assets/images/archive/`
  Old or replaced photos you want to keep out of the live flow.

Recommended flow:

1. Drop new product photos into `ready-for-sale`.
2. Refresh the image manifest so the admin sees them.
3. Build the draft product with those photos.
4. When the product is really live, move or copy the chosen photos into `live-products`.
5. Switch the product photo stage to `Live in market` so the admin points to the live folder.

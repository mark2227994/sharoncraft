Returns & Refunds

Our commitment to quality handmade goods.

We take pride in our artisan beadwork. If something isn't right, here is how we handle returns and refunds.

Return Eligibility

7-Day Return Window

We accept returns for items within 7 days of delivery. To be eligible for a return, the item must be unused, in its original condition, and in the original packaging.

Please note that custom or personalized beadwork orders cannot be returned unless they arrived damaged or defective.

Damaged Items

If your item arrives damaged

If your beadwork arrives damaged or broken, please contact us immediately on WhatsApp with a clear photo of the damage. We will arrange a replacement or issue a full refund at no additional cost to you.

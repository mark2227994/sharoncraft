# SharonCraft Visual Direction Checklist

Use this as the design filter when updating SharonCraft.

Goal:
- calm
- curated
- premium
- founder-led
- modern African editorial

Do more of this:

- use cleaner product presentation
- keep corners flatter and more architectural
- reduce decorative UI flourishes
- let strong images do more of the selling
- use warm neutrals with charcoal grounding
- keep layouts spacious and easy to scan
- write shorter, more confident section copy
- make landing pages feel collection-led

Color direction:

- warm ivory
- sand
- clay
- wood brown
- charcoal
- restrained terracotta
- small teal accents only when useful

Typography and copy:

- fewer words
- stronger headings
- less explanation
- more curation language
- less “helpful paragraph” density

Product grid direction:

- flatter card edges
- no decorative image overlays
- no logo or watermark on product thumbnails
- image-first scan
- cleaner borders and lighter shadows

Landing page direction:

- one strong editorial hero image
- one clear collection or intent
- shorter supporting copy
- trust through clarity, not too many flourishes

Image direction:

- cinematic realism
- modern African editorial
- Gen Z-friendly but premium
- warm daylight
- controlled black and charcoal accents
- no fake glossy CGI look

Avoid:

- too many gradients
- overly rounded cards everywhere
- decorative shapes that distract from products
- busy color noise
- fake stock-photo energy
- product photos with watermarks or logos baked in

Simple rule:

- calmer structure
- stronger imagery
- shorter copy
- cleaner product scan

# SharonCraft Sitewide AI Image Prompt Pack

Use this file to plan AI-generated non-product images across the rest of the SharonCraft website.

Core rule:
- keep real product photos for the shop, product pages, merchant feed, and product share pages
- use AI images for page heroes, lifestyle banners, trust/support visuals, and article hero atmosphere

This pack is organized in rollout batches so the site stays consistent.

## Global Direction

- highly realistic
- modern African editorial
- cinematic natural light
- Gen Z-friendly but premium
- warm, tactile, design-led
- controlled black and charcoal accents for richer premium contrast

Keywords to keep consistent:
- cinematic editorial photography
- modern African luxury
- youthful premium brand campaign
- art-directed realism
- directional natural light
- warm shadow depth
- sculptural composition
- matte black accents
- restrained earthy palette

Avoid:
- fake CGI gloss
- tourist-market stereotypes
- overly busy pattern overload
- product images pretending to be exact inventory
- plastic skin
- generic stock-photo energy

## Naming Advice

Keep filenames stable and descriptive so future swaps stay easy:

- `ai-landing-...`
- `ai-about-...`
- `ai-why-trust-...`
- `ai-article-...`

## Implementation Rule

When you are ready to use a generated image:

- update the hero image in the page body
- update `og:image`
- update `twitter:image`
- update any schema image reference if the page uses one

Do not replace real product thumbnails with AI images.

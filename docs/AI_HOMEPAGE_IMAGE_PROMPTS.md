## Quality Filter

Choose only images that have:

- believable skin texture
- correct hands and fingers
- realistic bead texture
- natural but intentional shadows
- strong focal composition
- clean negative space
- premium art direction
- youthful visual freshness
- elegant dark contrast
- warm color without turning orange

Reject any image that feels:

- too glossy
- too busy
- too safe or generic
- too costume-like
- too fake in skin, hands, beads, or surfaces
- more like stock photography than a brand campaign
- too mature or old-fashioned for a younger audience
